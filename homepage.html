The school perpetuates the fond memory of the Late Shri.Raj Bansi Prasad who was a great philanthropist and social worker, known for his zeal and benevolence for the weak and downtrodden.
His illustrious son, Mr. Subhash Chand Prasad, drawing inspiration from his father , endeavoured to realize his father’s dreams, and the two schools today stand as eloquent testimony to his sense of initiative, enterprise and mission. The main thrust of the school is to form, in the students, habits of diligence and piety, and ¤ Instill in them a sense of perseverance fortitude and hope. This, in short, sums up the whole philosophical foundations of the school and becomes The focus of all its educational efforts the entire curriculum of the school and its activities have been formulated, so that students may grow up as wholesome personalities with futurist attitudes and proactive temperaments.
